Things As They Are

Here you will meet explorers compelled to push the frontiers of thought, whether through rigorous profundity or topical breadth. You will consistently walk away with new ideas that you will wonder how you ever did without.

Jordan Peterson

A psychologist and researcher, Jordan Peterson began reaching out to wider audiences on-line long before he was swept up in Canada's culture war and thrust before global audiences. Since then, his unapologetic but exceptionally thoughtful tone coupled with his honest humanity and clinical concern has motivated millions to shun deception and look for solutions within themselves.

Lex Fridman

Born in Russia, Lex Fridman made his career in computer science before branching out to wider technical and cultural commentary on his channel. Something of a young Joe Rogan, Lex brings in a variety of expertise to consider the most salient issues of the day.
