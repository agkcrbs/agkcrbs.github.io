Content

Home page

Why all the terrible news?
Why the ugliness?
Whom does it benefit?

Find out this, and more.

Welcome to Wider Views, where information-starved news consumers like you can stretch beyond the straitjackets of traditional corporate programming.
This site tries to offer a diverse diet of viewpoints, but one thing it happily omits
is the craving for chaos, conflict, and contempt you will find from mainstream sources.

Explore the personalities below, and enjoy your visit.
