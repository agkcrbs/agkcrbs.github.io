Aaron Maté

Son of renowned thinker Gabor Maté, Aaron's passion was journalism. After confronting questions of his own Jewish identity, he proceeded to chase unpopular stories and defend unlikely victims far from his Canadian homeground. Now he contributes his keen, equal-opportunity critical analysis to television and on-line sites.

Russell Brand

Russell first made a name for himself with his high-speed brand of British quirkiness and excess before finding, as others before him, that comedy drags you toward the sharper problems of human nature. Now employing his sometimes rough humour on his on-line channels, he takes on all topics in his quest to bring out the best in himself and the world.
