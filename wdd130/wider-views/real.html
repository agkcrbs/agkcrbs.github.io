Straight Talk

When duty calls, you can count on these stalwarts to stand tall and weather any storm. They can be the best allies of the sincere, yet the worst critics of the haughty.

Doug Macgregor

Once called the country's "best warfighter", Doug Macgregor has served for decades as a soldier and advisor, though his need for newer, clearer thinking and a growing hunger to make peace repeatedly got in the way of his career. Now he shares his vast knowledge on-line and through video and telephone interviews.

Scott Ritter

Another career military expert, Scott Ritter's reputation was besmirched by his private behaviour -- but more importantly, by his penchant for lobbing inconvenient truths. Having seen the trouble with war up close, he is a vociferous reminder of its gravity in his interviews.
